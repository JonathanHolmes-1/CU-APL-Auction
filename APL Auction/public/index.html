How It Works

The APL Auction Experience

📝

1. Register

Submit your player profile with stats screenshot, experience level, and availability for the upcoming season.

✅

2. Get Approved

Our admin team reviews and approves your profile, setting your base price based on your stats and experience.

🔨

3. Get Auctioned

During the live auction, franchise teams bid on you using their purse. Highest bidder wins your services.

🏏

4. Play!

Join your franchise team and compete in the APL Season 1 tournament. Glory awaits!
